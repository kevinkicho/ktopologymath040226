In the 2-adic world, …1111 (all ones in binary) equals -1. Powers of p are small. The p-adic integers ℤ_p form a Cantor-like tree. The ultrametric inequality |x+y|_p ≤ max(|x|_p, |y|_p) is stronger than the triangle inequality.

The adele ring 𝔸_ℚ = ℝ × ∏'_p ℚ_p combines all completions of ℚ simultaneously. A rational number embeds diagonally into all components. The product formula ∏_p |x|_p · |x|_∞ = 1 is a global conservation law.

*ℝ contains infinitesimals ε with 0 < ε < 1/n for ALL n ∈ ℕ, and infinite numbers ω > n for all n. The derivative f'(x) = st(Δf/Δx) where Δx is genuinely infinitesimal. Leibniz was right all along!
