Penrose–Carter Diagrams

A conformal compactification maps all of spacetime (including infinities) into a finite bounded region, preserving causal structure. Null rays always travel at 45°.
  • $r_s = 2GM/c^2$ — Schwarzschild radius
  • $T_H = \hbar c^3 / (8\pi G M k_B)$ — Hawking temperature
  • $ds^2 = -(1-r_s/r)c^2dt^2 + ...$ — Schwarzschild metric
Kruskal coordinates: $T^2 - X^2 = (1 - r/r_s)e^{r/r_s}$. The maximal extension reveals four regions: exterior (I), black hole interior (II), white hole (III), second exterior (IV).
Causal boundaries: $i^+$ = future timelike infinity, $i^-$ = past timelike infinity, $i^0$ = spacelike infinity, $\mathscr{J}^+$ = future null infinity, $\mathscr{J}^-$ = past null infinity.
Event horizon: The boundary of the causal past of $\mathscr{J}^+$ — no signal from inside can reach outside. A global, teleological concept requiring knowledge of the entire future.
Singularity: $r = 0$ in Schwarzschild — spacelike, shown as a jagged line at the top. Every future-directed timelike/null curve inside the horizon hits it.

Hawking Radiation

Near the event horizon, quantum vacuum fluctuations produce virtual particle–antiparticle pairs. Occasionally one falls in (negative energy w.r.t. infinity) and the other escapes — carrying real energy away from the black hole.
  • $S_{BH} = k_B A / (4\ell_P^2)$ — Bekenstein–Hawking entropy
  • $L \propto M^{-2}$ — Hawking luminosity
  • $\tau \sim M^3$ — evaporation lifetime
Information paradox: Pure state collapses → mixed thermal radiation? Unitarity violated? Hawking (1976) vs modern view: information scrambled, released near Page time $t_P \sim M^3/2$.
Firewall paradox (AMPS, 2012): Monogamy of entanglement forces a choice — smooth horizon or unitarity. No consensus yet; one of Penrose's deep concerns about quantum gravity.

Kerr Geometry

Rotating black holes described by mass $M$ and spin $a = J/M$ (with $a \le M$ in geometrized units). The ergosphere is a region where no observer can remain stationary — frame dragging becomes superluminal.
  • $r_\pm = M \pm \sqrt{M^2 - a^2}$ — horizons
  • $r_{erg} = M + \sqrt{M^2 - a^2\cos^2\theta}$ — ergosphere
  • $A = 8\pi M r_+$ — Kerr horizon area

Four Laws of BH Thermodynamics

ZEROTH LAW
Surface gravity $\kappa$ is constant over the horizon — analogous to temperature uniformity in thermal equilibrium.
FIRST LAW
$dM = \dfrac{\kappa}{8\pi}dA + \Omega\, dJ + \Phi\, dQ$
SECOND LAW
$dA \ge 0$ — the horizon area never decreases (classically). Hawking's area theorem, 1971. Analogue of entropy increase.
THIRD LAW
$\kappa \to 0$ is unattainable in finite steps — extremal black holes ($a = M$) cannot be reached.
Penrose process: A particle splits inside the ergosphere; one fragment has negative energy, falls in, the other escapes with energy $> $ original. Maximum extraction: $\approx 29\%$ of total mass ($1 - 1/\sqrt{2}$).
Irreducible mass: $M_{irr} = \sqrt{A/(16\pi)}$. The Penrose process reduces $J$ but $M_{irr}$ can only increase.

Conformal Compactification

A conformal transformation $g_{\mu\nu}\to\Omega^2 g_{\mu\nu}$ preserves angles and hence the 45° null-ray structure, but squeezes infinite spacetime into a bounded diamond. Light cones remain at 45° everywhere.
  • $r_s = 2GM/c^2$ — Schwarzschild radius
  • $r_\pm = M \pm \sqrt{M^2-Q^2}$ — RN horizons
  • $r_\pm = M \pm \sqrt{M^2-a^2}$ — Kerr horizons
SPACELIKE vs TIMELIKE SINGULARITY
Schwarzschild $r=0$ is spacelike — drawn as a horizontal jagged bar, unavoidable for all interior observers. Kerr/RN $r=0$ is timelike — drawn vertically, allowing timelike curves to avoid it (though Cauchy horizon instability likely forecloses this).
CAUCHY HORIZON INSTABILITY
The inner horizon $r_-$ of RN/Kerr is a Cauchy horizon: initial data does not uniquely determine evolution beyond it. Penrose's Strong Cosmic Censorship (SCC) conjecture: generic perturbations make it singular (blue-shift instability, Dafermos-Luk 2017 partial result).
OPPENHEIMER-SNYDER COLLAPSE
Homogeneous dust collapse (1939): the star interior matches a closed FLRW universe; exterior is Schwarzschild. No white hole or second exterior in the physical diagram — those are artefacts of the eternal maximally extended Schwarzschild solution.
COSMIC CENSORSHIP (PENROSE 1969)
Weak form: no naked singularities visible from $\mathscr{J}^+$. Strong form: every inextendible spacetime satisfying the dominant energy condition is globally hyperbolic. Both remain open conjectures despite major progress.
Topology changes: Traversable wormholes (Einstein-Rosen bridges in eternal Schwarzschild) would require exotic matter violating energy conditions. ER = EPR (Maldacena-Susskind): entangled black holes are connected by a non-traversable wormhole.

Hawking Radiation (1974)

Hawking's QFT-in-curved-spacetime calculation: the Bogoliubov transformation between in-vacuum (past null infinity) and out-vacuum (future null infinity) is non-trivial, mixing positive and negative frequency modes. The black hole radiates as a blackbody at $T_H$.
  • $T_H = \dfrac{\hbar c^3}{8\pi G M k_B}$
  • $t_{evap} \approx \dfrac{5120\pi G^2 M^3}{\hbar c^4}$
  • $L_{Hawk} \propto \hbar c^6/(G^2 M^2)$
BOGOLIUBOV TRANSFORMATION
In-modes $\{f_\omega\}$ and out-modes $\{p_\omega\}$ are related by $\hat a_{out} = \alpha \hat a_{in} + \beta^* \hat a^\dagger_{in}$. When $\beta\ne0$, particles are created. The thermal spectrum arises from the exact Bogolubov coefficients $|\beta_{\omega\omega'}|^2 = (e^{2\pi\omega/\kappa}-1)^{-1}$.
GREYBODY FACTORS
The BH is not a perfect blackbody; the potential barrier around the horizon partially reflects outgoing modes, yielding greybody factors $\Gamma_\ell(\omega)$ — frequency-dependent transmission coefficients. Affects spectrum shape significantly for small BHs.
INFORMATION PARADOX
If a pure quantum state collapses to a BH and then evaporates to mixed thermal radiation, unitarity is violated. Hawking (1976) accepted this; modern consensus (via AdS/CFT) says information must be preserved — released near the Page time $t_{Page} \approx t_{evap}/2$ via subtle correlations.
FIREWALL PARADOX (AMPS 2012)
Almheiri, Marolf, Polchinski, Sully: monogamy of entanglement forces a choice — either (a) the horizon is smooth (infalling observer sees nothing special) or (b) early and late Hawking radiation are maximally entangled (unitarity). You cannot have both. One resolution requires "ER = EPR" or non-locality.
ISLAND FORMULA (2019)
$S_{rad} = \min\operatorname{ext}_{\mathcal{I}}\left[S_{bulk}(\mathcal{R}\cup\mathcal{I}) + \frac{A(\partial\mathcal{I})}{4G\hbar}\right]$. An "island" region inside the BH contributes to the radiation entropy, reproducing the Page curve and restoring unitarity in JT gravity models (Penington; Almheiri et al.).

Kerr Geometry (1963)

Roy Kerr's exact vacuum solution for a rotating mass: the unique (by uniqueness theorems) stationary, axisymmetric BH solution. Described by mass $M$ and angular momentum $J=aM$ with $|a|\le M$ (geometrized units $G=c=1$).
  • $ds^2 = -\left(1-\frac{r_s r}{\rho^2}\right)dt^2 - \frac{2r_s r a\sin^2\theta}{\rho^2}dt\,d\phi + ...$
  • $r_\pm = M \pm \sqrt{M^2-a^2}$ — horizons
  • $r_{erg}(\theta) = M + \sqrt{M^2 - a^2\cos^2\theta}$
  • $r_{ISCO} = r_{ISCO}(a)$ — varies 1M to 9M
ERGOSPHERE
Between $r_+$ and $r_{erg}(\theta)$: the static limit surface where $g_{tt}=0$. Inside, no observer can remain stationary — they are dragged in the rotation direction at $v>c$ relative to infinity. Physical objects can exist here (unlike inside $r_+$); hence energy extraction is possible.
PENROSE PROCESS
A particle enters ergosphere with energy $E_0>0$, splits; fragment 1 has $E_1<0$ (negative energy states exist in ergosphere), falls into BH. Fragment 2 escapes with $E_2 = E_0-E_1 > E_0$. The extracted energy comes from rotational kinetic energy; maximum efficiency: $1-1/\sqrt{2}\approx 29\%$ of irreducible mass.
BLANDFORD-ZNAJEK MECHANISM
Electromagnetic analog of the Penrose process: magnetic field lines threading the horizon extract rotational energy via Poynting flux, powering relativistic jets in AGN and GRBs. Efficiency can exceed the Penrose process mechanically. Key mechanism in M87* jet (EHT, 2019).
ISCO (INNERMOST STABLE CIRCULAR ORBIT)
$r_{ISCO} = 6M$ for Schwarzschild, $r_{ISCO} \to M$ for maximally prograde Kerr, $r_{ISCO} \to 9M$ for maximally retrograde. ISCO spin determines radiative efficiency: $\eta = 1-\sqrt{1-2/(3r_{ISCO}/M)}$, up to 42% for extremal Kerr vs 6% for Schwarzschild.
FRAME DRAGGING (LENSE-THIRRING)
Angular velocity of ZAMOs (zero-angular-momentum observers): $\Omega_{LT} = -g_{t\phi}/g_{\phi\phi} = 2Mar/(r^2+a^2)^2+...$. Measured by Gravity Probe B (2011) in Earth's field: 37.2 mas/yr (geodetic) + 6.6 mas/yr (frame drag). For stellar-mass BHs, GW spin measurements (LIGO) probe this.

Gravitational Wave Signatures

LIGO's first detection (GW150914, Sept 14 2015): a 0.2-second chirp reaching 150 Hz, from 36+29 → 62 M☉ merger at 410 Mpc. Three solar masses radiated as gravitational waves — the brightest observed event in the universe at that moment.
  • $\dot P = -\dfrac{32G^4}{5c^5}\dfrac{m_1^2 m_2^2(m_1+m_2)}{r^5}$
  • $\mathcal{M} = \dfrac{(m_1 m_2)^{3/5}}{(m_1+m_2)^{1/5}}$ — chirp mass
  • $f_{chirp}(t) \propto \mathcal{M}^{-5/8}(t_c-t)^{-3/8}$
THREE PHASES
Inspiral: PN (post-Newtonian) regime; frequency sweeps up as $f\propto (t_c-t)^{-3/8}$. Merger: fully non-linear NR; amplitude peaks. Ringdown: perturbed final BH radiates quasinormal modes; dominant QNM $f_{220} = (c^3/2\pi GM_{fin})\times 0.373(1-0.32\chi^{0.57})$.
QUADRUPOLE FORMULA
$h_+ = \frac{4G}{c^4 r}\frac{d^2 I_{xx}}{dt^2}$ where $I_{ij}$ is the trace-free quadrupole. For circular orbit: $h = (4G\mathcal{M}/c^2 r)(G\mathcal{M}\pi f/c^3)^{2/3}$. GW150914 peak strain $\sim 10^{-21}$ — Earth deformed by $\sim 10^{-18}$ m.
GW150914 PARAMETERS
$m_1=36_{-4}^{+5}M_\odot$, $m_2=29_{-4}^{+4}M_\odot$, $M_{fin}=62_{-4}^{+4}M_\odot$, $\chi_{eff}\approx-0.06$, $d_L=410_{-180}^{+160}$ Mpc, $z\approx0.09$. Radiated energy: $3.0^{+0.5}_{-0.5}M_\odot c^2 \approx 5\times10^{47}$ J.
QUASINORMAL MODES (RINGDOWN)
Perturbed Kerr BH has discrete complex frequencies $\omega_{lmn} = \omega_R + i\omega_I$. Real part = oscillation, imaginary = damping. The (2,2,0) mode dominates. BH spectroscopy: measuring multiple QNMs tests no-hair theorem (Dreyer et al. 2004, now achievable with LIGO O3).
LIGO / VIRGO / KAGRA
As of O3 (2021): 90 confirmed events in GWTC-3. Mass gap events (GW190814: 23+2.6 M☉), neutron star mergers (GW170817 — multi-messenger), intermediate mass BH hints. LISA (2035): mHz band, massive BH mergers, EMRIs. Einstein Telescope: 10× sensitivity.

BH Thermodynamics & Information

Bekenstein (1972) proposed $S_{BH}\propto A$ on entropic grounds; Hawking (1974) confirmed with $T_H=\kappa/2\pi$ (in units $\hbar=k_B=1$), fixing the coefficient: $S_{BH} = k_B A/(4\ell_P^2)$.
  • $S_{BH} = \dfrac{k_B A}{4\ell_P^2} = \dfrac{4\pi k_B G M^2}{\hbar c}$
  • $T_H = \dfrac{\hbar c^3}{8\pi G M k_B}$
  • $S_{Bek} \le \dfrac{2\pi E R}{\hbar c}$ — Bekenstein bound
FOUR LAWS OF BH MECHANICS
0th: $\kappa=\mathrm{const}$ on horizon (analogue: $T$=const in equilibrium).
1st: $dM = \frac{\kappa}{8\pi}dA + \Omega\,dJ + \Phi\,dQ$ (analogue: $dE=TdS+...$).
2nd: $dA\ge0$ classically (analogue: $dS\ge0$). Hawking 1971.
3rd: $\kappa\to0$ unattainable in finite steps. Extremal BH $a=M$ is the limit.
PAGE CURVE & PAGE TIME
Don Page (1993): if evaporation is unitary, the entanglement entropy $S_{rad}$ of Hawking radiation first rises (BH emits entangled pairs), peaks at Page time $t_{Page}\approx t_{evap}/2$ when $S_{rad}=S_{BH}$, then decreases back to zero as the BH disappears. This is the purification curve of a unitary process.
HOLOGRAPHIC PRINCIPLE
't Hooft and Susskind (1993): the maximum entropy in a region is $A/4\ell_P^2$ — determined by the bounding surface area, not volume. Implies the universe's degrees of freedom live on a lower-dimensional boundary. Realized precisely in AdS/CFT (Maldacena 1997).
GENERALIZED SECOND LAW
Bekenstein: $S_{total} = S_{matter} + S_{BH}$ never decreases. Consistent with Hawking radiation: as BH loses mass ($S_{BH}$ decreases), emitted radiation entropy increases by a larger amount. Proven for semiclassical gravity by Wall (2011).
SUSSKIND'S "BLACK HOLE WARS"
20-year debate (1976–2004): Hawking argued information is destroyed; Susskind and 't Hooft insisted unitarity holds. Resolved by AdS/CFT (Maldacena): a CFT is manifestly unitary; its bulk dual BH must also be unitary. Hawking conceded in 2004. The precise mechanism of information release remains unsolved.
Island formula (2019): Penington; Almheiri et al. — using replica wormholes in JT gravity, reproduces the Page curve exactly from gravitational path integral, without invoking string theory explicitly.
