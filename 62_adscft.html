Poincaré AdS

$$ds^2 = \frac{L^2}{z^2}(dz^2 + dx_\mu dx^\mu)$$

The conformal boundary is at $z\to 0$. The bulk is the interior of the disk.

Geodesics

In the Poincaré disk model, geodesics are circular arcs orthogonal to the boundary circle, or diameters.

Global AdS₃

$$ds^2 = L^2(-\cosh^2\rho\, dt^2 + d\rho^2 + \sinh^2\rho\, d\phi^2)$$

Concentric rings represent constant-$\rho$ slices. The boundary is $\rho\to\infty$.

Conformal Boundary

The boundary $\partial(\text{AdS})$ inherits a conformal structure — a $(d-1)$-dimensional conformal field theory lives there. This is the CFT in AdS/CFT.

GKPW Formula

$$Z_\text{CFT}[J] = Z_\text{grav}[\phi\big|_\partial = J]$$

The partition function of the CFT with source $J$ equals the bulk gravity partition function with boundary condition $\phi|_\partial = J$.

Field-Operator Map

Each bulk field $\phi$ of mass $m$ corresponds to a CFT operator $\mathcal{O}$ of conformal dimension $\Delta$:

$$m^2 L^2 = \Delta(\Delta - d)$$

Two-Point Function

$$\langle\mathcal{O}(x)\mathcal{O}(0)\rangle = \frac{C_\Delta}{|x|^{2\Delta}}$$

The bulk field profile encodes the boundary correlator. Near $z=0$: $\phi \sim z^{d-\Delta} J + z^\Delta \langle\mathcal{O}\rangle$.

Holographic Renormalization

Near-boundary expansion extracts divergences and renormalizes them — mapping to CFT counterterms on the boundary.

Ryu-Takayanagi Formula

$$S_A = \frac{\text{Area}(\gamma_A)}{4G_N}$$

The entanglement entropy of a boundary region $A$ equals the area of the minimal bulk surface $\gamma_A$ homologous to $A$.

Strong Subadditivity

$$S(AB) + S(BC) \geq S(B) + S(ABC)$$

Automatically satisfied by the RT formula via geometry — a beautiful consistency check.

Island Formula

$$S_\text{gen} = \frac{\text{Area}(\partial I)}{4G_N} + S_\text{bulk}$$

For evaporating black holes, an "island" contribution resolves the information paradox and gives a Page curve consistent with unitarity.

BTZ Black Hole

$$ds^2 = -\frac{r^2-r_+^2}{L^2}dt^2 + \frac{L^2 dr^2}{r^2-r_+^2} + r^2 d\phi^2$$

The BTZ black hole is an exact solution of 3D gravity with $\Lambda<0$. It has a horizon at $r=r_+$.

Hawking Temperature

$$T_H = \frac{r_+}{2\pi L^2}$$

The dual CFT is in a thermal state at temperature $T_H$. The inverse temperature $\beta=1/T_H$ is the circumference of the thermal circle.

Bekenstein-Hawking Entropy

$$S_{BH} = \frac{2\pi r_+}{4G_N}$$

This matches the thermal entropy of the dual CFT via the Cardy formula — a perfect holographic check.

Penrose Diagram

The maximally extended BTZ spacetime has two asymptotic regions connected by an Einstein-Rosen bridge (wormhole) — dual to the thermofield double state in the CFT.

Holographic RG

The AdS radial direction $z$ maps to the RG energy scale $\mu\sim 1/z$. Moving into the bulk = flowing to the IR.

Callan-Symanzik Equation

$$\mu\frac{\partial g}{\partial\mu} = \beta(g)$$

Holographic Beta Function

$$\beta(g) \propto z\,\partial_z \phi$$

The bulk scalar profile encodes the running of the coupling constant. Fixed points of the RG = critical points in AdS.

c-Theorem

$$\frac{dc}{d\mu} \leq 0$$

The number of degrees of freedom decreases along the RG flow. Holographically, this corresponds to the monotone decrease of the AdS area element toward the IR.

Fixed Points

UV fixed point (boundary, $z\to 0$): conformal field theory. IR fixed point (deep bulk, $z\to\infty$): another CFT or a gapped phase.

MERA & Holography

The Multi-scale Entanglement Renormalization Ansatz (MERA) has a tree structure that mirrors the geometry of Anti-de Sitter space.

Entanglement Scaling

$$S \sim \frac{c}{3}\ln|A|$$

MERA naturally reproduces the logarithmic entanglement entropy of a 1+1D CFT.

HaPPY Code

The Harlow-Almheiri-Papadodimas-Preskill-Yoshida code tiles the Poincaré disk with pentagons. Each pentagon is a perfect tensor — a model of bulk locality and quantum error correction.

$$|\psi\rangle_\text{logical} \longleftrightarrow \text{boundary state}$$

ER = EPR

Einstein-Rosen bridges (wormholes) are dual to Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen pairs (entanglement). Tensor network bonds = EPR pairs = ER bridge.

Perfect Tensors

A tensor is perfect if tracing out any half of its indices gives a maximally mixed state. The HaPPY code uses these to realize holographic quantum error correction.

't Hooft Limit

$$N\to\infty,\quad \lambda = g^2 N = \text{fixed}$$

In this limit, only planar Feynman diagrams contribute. Non-planar diagrams are suppressed by $1/N^{2g}$ where $g$ is the genus.

Topological Expansion

$$F = \sum_{g=0}^\infty N^{2-2g} f_g(\lambda)$$

The free energy is a genus expansion analogous to the string loop expansion, with $1/N$ playing the role of the string coupling $g_s$.

Weak/Strong Duality

AdS/CFT is a strong/weak duality: when $\lambda\gg 1$ (strong coupling in CFT), the bulk is weakly curved and classical gravity applies. When $\lambda\ll 1$, the bulk geometry is highly stringy.

Type IIB on AdS₅×S⁵

$$\mathcal{N}=4\;\text{SYM} \;\longleftrightarrow\; \text{IIB on AdS}_5\times S^5$$

The original Maldacena duality: $\mathcal{N}=4$ super Yang-Mills with gauge group $SU(N)$ is dual to Type IIB superstring theory on $\text{AdS}_5\times S^5$ with $N$ units of 5-form flux.

KSS Bound

$$\frac{\eta}{s} \geq \frac{1}{4\pi}$$

Holography predicts this universal lower bound on the viscosity-to-entropy ratio, saturated by strongly coupled plasmas like the QGP at RHIC/LHC.

Holographic Superconductor

Below a critical temperature $T_c$, a charged scalar condenses in the bulk — dual to Cooper pairing on the boundary. The order parameter vanishes above $T_c$.

Strange Metal

Holographic strange metals exhibit linear-in-$T$ resistivity $\rho\sim T$, unlike Fermi liquid $\rho\sim T^2$. This matches observations in cuprate superconductors.

Holographic Complexity

$$\mathcal{C}_V \sim \frac{V}{G_N L}$$

The complexity of the boundary state equals the volume of the Einstein-Rosen bridge, which grows linearly behind the horizon — even after the Hawking radiation has thermalized.
