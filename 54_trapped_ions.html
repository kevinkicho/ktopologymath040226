Trapped Ions & Cavity QED

2012 Nobel Physics · Haroche · Wineland
Trap Parameters
Stability—
Move q above 0.908 or a outside the stability triangle to see the ion escape.
Paul Trap
$$a_u = \frac{-4eU}{m\Omega^2 r_0^2}$$
$$q_u = \frac{2eV}{m\Omega^2 r_0^2}$$
Stability requires $(a,q)$ inside the first Mathieu stability zone: $q < 0.908$. The ion undergoes fast micromotion at Ω plus slow secular oscillation.
System
Vacuum Rabi splitting: even with n=0 photons, the atom oscillates due to coupling to the vacuum field — Ω₀ = g√1.
Jaynes-Cummings
$$H = \hbar g(a\sigma_+ + a^\dagger\sigma_-)$$
$$\Omega_n = g\sqrt{n+1}$$
The Bloch sphere shows the atom state. At $n=0$, the vacuum Rabi frequency $\Omega_0 = g$ — atom couples to zero-point field fluctuations.
Cat State
Red = negative Wigner values — a purely quantum signature. Decoherence kills the fringes, producing a classical statistical mixture.
Wigner Function
$$W(\alpha) = \frac{2}{\pi}\text{Tr}[\hat{\rho}\,\hat{D}(\alpha)\hat{P}\hat{D}^\dagger(\alpha)]$$
Negative W values are a definitive marker of non-classical states. The cat state $|\alpha\rangle + |-\alpha\rangle$ shows interference fringes that decohere exponentially fast in a real environment.

Linear Paul Trap
$$\ddot{u}+\frac{\Omega^2}{4}(a-2q\cos\Omega t)u=0$$
Mathieu equation — secular + micromotion
$$\omega_\text{sec}\approx\frac{\Omega}{2}\sqrt{a+\frac{q^2}{2}}$$
Pseudopotential secular frequency
$$\eta=k\sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2m\omega_z}}\ll 1$$
Lamb-Dicke parameter — required for sideband cooling
$$k_BT_D=\frac{\hbar\gamma}{2}$$
Doppler cooling limit
Platforms: NIST (Be⁺, Mg⁺), Innsbruck (Ca⁺), IonQ (Yb⁺), Quantinuum (H₁/H₂). Penning traps use static B-field instead of RF — ideal for 2D crystal arrays.

MS Gate Parameters
Gate fidelity—
Entanglement—
Mølmer-Sørensen Gate
$$H_\text{MS}=\Omega(\sigma_1^++\sigma_1^-)(\hat{a}e^{i\delta t}+\hat{a}^\dagger e^{-i\delta t})$$
Bichromatic drive — geometric phase gate
$$|\Psi\rangle = \frac{|\!\uparrow\uparrow\rangle + i|\!\downarrow\downarrow\rangle}{\sqrt{2}}$$
Bell state after π/2 MS pulse
2-qubit fidelity record: 99.9% (Innsbruck 2022, Oxford 2023).

All-to-all connectivity: any pair of ions share the motional bus — no routing overhead.

Mid-circuit measurement allows real-time quantum error correction.
Ising Model
Phase—
Porras-Cirac Ising
$$H=-\sum_{i<j}\frac{J_0}{|i-j|^\alpha}\sigma_i^x\sigma_j^x - h\sum_i\sigma_i^z$$
Variable-range Ising model, α ∈ [0,3]
$$J_{ij}\propto|i-j|^{-\alpha}$$
α=0: all-to-all · α=1: Coulomb · α=3: dipolar
Monroe group (2021): observed discrete time crystal in 57 Yb⁺ ions — first experimental time crystal.

Quantum advantage: frustrated magnets and gauge theories are classically intractable at N~50+.

Penning trap: 2D crystal of ~300 ions for analog simulation.
Platform Filter
QCCD: Quantum Charge-Coupled Device — shuttle ions between trapping zones for scalable multi-module architectures.
