Thurston's Program
Geometrization
Every compact 3-manifold decomposes into pieces each admitting one of 8 model geometries. The "generic" geometry is $\mathbb{H}^3$.
Dehn Surgery
$M(p/q)$ = Dehn fill on slope $p/q$.
All but finitely many fillings remain hyperbolic (Thurston).
Ricci Flow
$\dfrac{\partial g_{ij}}{\partial t} = -2R_{ij}$
Perelman (2003): Ricci flow with surgery proves geometrization (and thus Poincaré conjecture).
