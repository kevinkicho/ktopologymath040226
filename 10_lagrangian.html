Symmetry
Time translation ($t\to t+\epsilon$):
Conserved = Energy $H = p\dot q - L$.

Break by $V\to V(1+\epsilon\sin t)$ → $\dot H\neq 0$.
Space translation ($q\to q+\epsilon$):
Conserved = Momentum $p=\partial L/\partial\dot q$.

Break by adding $V(q)$ → $\dot p=-\partial V/\partial q\neq 0$.
Rotational symmetry:
Conserved = Angular Momentum $L=r\times p$.

Break by non-central term → $\dot L\neq 0$.
